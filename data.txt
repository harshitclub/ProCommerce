Super Admin Api's:-
1. /super-admin/register (Register Super Admin)
2. /super-admin/login (Login Super Admin)
3. /super-admin/profile (Get Profile)
4. /super-admin/users (Get All User's)
5. /super-admin/user/:id (Get Particular User)
6. /super-admin/user/:id/cart (Get User's Cart Items)
7. /super-admin
